The Hyper-Local Opportunity

This section outlines the core problem identified during a 2-year study in Namilyango, Gwafu, and Njerere. It highlights the friction points of traditional grocery shopping and presents QuickStore's digital solution. The goal is to establish the foundation of our business thesis.

🌧️

❌ The Problem

  • ▪️ Residents face immense friction maneuvering crowded local markets.
  • ▪️ Intense weather conditions frequently disrupt daily household provisioning.
  • ▪️ Lack of reliable logistics for fresh agricultural products (tomatoes, onions, meats).
📱

✅ The QuickStore Solution

  • ▪️ A hyper-local app connecting households directly to nearby vendors.
  • ▪️ Integrated Mobile Money (MoMo) for seamless, cashless transactions.
  • ▪️ Fast, trackable home delivery bringing the market to the consumer's doorstep.

Decentralized Logistics Model

Solving the "Boda Dilemma". Instead of managing a fleet of riders (which causes high cash burn), QuickStore shifts fulfillment to the suppliers. This section breaks down the step-by-step transaction flow. Click the steps below to explore how an order moves from cart to doorstep.

Customer Places Order

The resident opens QuickStore, selects a local vendor (e.g., Mama Njerere Fresh Produce), adds tomatoes and onions to the cart, enters their address, and completes payment instantly via MTN/Airtel Mobile Money.

Technical MVP Scope

To launch quickly in Namilyango and test assumptions, the MVP must be strictly scoped. This section categorizes the exact features required for launch across the three core applications (Customer, Supplier, Admin), built on a Nuxt 3 & PostgreSQL stack.

🔍 Browse Suppliers

View local vendors and their grocery catalogs.

🛒 Shopping Cart

Add items, adjust quantities, view total breakdown.

💳 Checkout & MoMo

Enter delivery address and pay via MTN/Airtel API.

📍 Order Tracking

Status updates (Received, Prep, Out, Delivered).

📦 Manage Products

List tomatoes, onions, meat with prices and images.

🔔 Receive Orders

Real-time notifications for incoming customer orders.

📝 Accept/Reject

Confirm ability to fulfill based on current inventory.

🛵 Status Updates

Manually trigger "Out for Delivery" when boda leaves.

✅ Approve Suppliers

Vet and onboard reliable vendors in target areas.

📊 View Orders

Global view of all system transactions and statuses.

💰 Commission Tracking

Monitor the accumulated 1,500 UGX platform fees.
